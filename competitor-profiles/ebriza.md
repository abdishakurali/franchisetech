## Key Features

### POS (all plans)

- Native app on iOS/Android tablet or phone
- Counter, table, and food-truck service modes
- Multiple payment methods, split bills, quick discounts
- CUI on receipt, product voids
- Soft POS integrations (ING SoftPOS, GP Tom, Viva) — NFC on same device
- Customer display + transparent tips
- Real-time reporting via Ebriza Operations mobile app
- e-Factura / SPV send & receive
- Cash register (registru de casă) — auto from fiscal receipts, XLS/PDF export
- Room/area configuration (săli)
- Delivery room in POS for integrated orders
- Promotion manager (Happy Hour, 1+1, client-specific, recurring)

### Gestiune (Premium + Titanium only)

- Real-time stock, weighted average cost (CMP)
- SPV invoice import → automatic NIR
- Supplier orders (email, SMS, WhatsApp)
- Consumption vouchers (bon de consum)
- Inter-location stock transfers
- Physical inventory from mobile (Ebriza Operations)
- Supplier balance tracking
- Low-stock alerts
- Saga accounting export (add-on 39 €/mo)

### Integrations (marketed heavily)

Delivery: Bolt Food, Glovo, Wolt
Online orders: Gloria Food
Payments: GP Tom, ING SoftPOS
Accounting: Saga
Fleet/delivery management partners listed
Open API — free access advertised

### Offline Claim

> **"Offline Mode"** (pricing comparison table): *Fără internet, permite printarea bonurilor fiscale, nu încarcă datele în cloud*

Homepage FAQ expands: fiscal receipts and order tickets print over **local network** without active internet; sales data syncs when back online.

**Important:** Offline is app-based with local fiscal printing — not a browser POS. Cloud sync deferred until reconnect.

## Strengths vs franchisetech

Context: franchisetech targets **browser POS for SMB cafés** (1–3 locations), honest daily close, margins without add-on traps.

### Where Ebriza is strong

| Strength | Detail |
|----------|--------|
| **Mature HoReCa ecosystem** | Delivery aggregators, KDS, customer display, soft POS, multi-channel orders in one POS |
| **Tiered scale path** | Pro → Premium → Titanium for growing chains and high delivery volume |
| **Native mobile/tablet UX** | Polished iOS/Android apps; table-side ordering on phones |
| **Integration breadth** | Bolt, Glovo, Wolt, Gloria Food, Saga, NFC payments — marketed as market leader |
| **Offline fiscal printing** | Can print fiscal receipts locally without internet (sync later) |
| **Unlimited users** | No per-seat pricing |
| **7/7 support** | Free chat/phone, 2–5 min response claimed |
| **No setup fee** | Free video onboarding |
| **Multi-location** | One-click new location, location-specific pricing |

### Where Ebriza has gaps vs franchisetech (SMB café angle)

| Gap | franchisetech advantage |
|-----|-------------------------|
| **Add-on cost stacking** | Insights +19 €, KDS +19 €, Saga +39 € on top of base — Z/sales reporting effectively upsold on Pro |
| **Gestiune locked to 99 €+** | franchisetech Pro (79 €) includes stock, recipes, NIR, margin reports — no jump to Premium tier |
| **Per-order delivery fees on Pro** | 0,06 €/order on Pro vs bundled on higher tiers — hurts delivery-light cafés comparing headline price |
| **Native app + hardware dependency** | Requires tablet/phone install; extra devices 29 €/mo; franchisetech is browser-first — any PC/tablet, no app store |
| **Card required for trial install** | franchisetech trial 15 days without card |
| **DIY implementation** | Hours–3 days self-setup; franchisetech targets faster café onboarding |
| **Insights not in base Pro** | franchisetech Starter includes sales, Z-report, VAT reports at 49 € |
| **FiscalNet local agent** | franchisetech integrates FiscalNet for Romanian fiscal hardware where configured; Ebriza uses Datecs/Partner fiscal printers via their app stack |
| **Complexity for 1-location café** | Marketplace + add-ons + tiers optimized for full HoReCa stack — overkill for "close the day with truth" |
| **TVA / margin clarity** | Ebriza pushes advanced reporting to Insights add-on or Titanium; franchisetech leads with owner-trust reports in base plan |

## Head-to-Head Price Reality (1-location café)

| Scenario | Ebriza typical | franchisetech |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| POS + daily reports only | Pro 49 € + Insights 19 € = **68 €+** | Starter **49 €** (reports included) |
| POS + stock + NIR | Premium **99 €** | Pro **79 €** |
| POS + KDS + Saga | Pro 49 + 19 + 19 + 39 = **126 €+** | Pro 79 € + evaluate KDS needs separately |
| Delivery-heavy | Premium 99 € (1.500 orders) or per-order on Pro | Not core ICP — Ebriza wins on integrations |

## Positioning Summary

**Ebriza** = Romanian cloud HoReCa platform with strong delivery/integration story, native tablet POS, modular add-ons. Best fit: restaurants with delivery volume, multi-location chains, operators who want Bolt/Glovo/Wolt in one POS.

**franchisetech** = Browser POS for small Romanian food businesses who want transparent all-in pricing, daily Z/sales/VAT without Insights upsell, stock/recipes at Pro without Premium tier, and FiscalNet-friendly fiscal workflow — "close the day with truth" over integration breadth.
