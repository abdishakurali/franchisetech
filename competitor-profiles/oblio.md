## Snapshot

| Attribute | Value |
|-----------|-------|

| **Claimed users** | 150.000+ firme si PFA-uri |
| **Claimed volume** | 40M+ facturi/an |
| **Price (paid)** | €29/an (~€2.49/lună) — plan unic „Nelimitat” |
| **Trial** | 1 an gratuit full; ≤3 documente/lună = gratuit pe viață |
| **Delivery** | Cloud SaaS, fără instalare locală |
| **Compliance hooks** | e-Factura, e-Transport, SAF-T — marketed as gratuit |

Oblio is **not** a restaurant operations platform. It is a **Romanian invoicing and accounting-adjacent stock system** with optional fiscal-register integration, priced aggressively for broad SMB/PFA adoption.

|---|---:|---:|---:|
| **Price** | €29/year (~€2.49/mo) | €49/month | €79/month |
| **Annual cost** | ~€29 | ~€588 | ~€948 |

Oblio’s price is a **category anchor** for Romanian owners who think in „facturare + e-Factura” budgets, not hospitality ops software. franchisetech competes on **outcome** (close the day with truth), not on invoice volume.

**Oblio freemium wedge:** 3 docs/month forever catches micro-PFA and side businesses franchisetech does not target.

## Feature map

### What Oblio does well (strengths)

1. **e-Factura / SPV at commodity price** — SPV connect in 5 seconds, bulk send, e-Transport UIT, SAF-T export; all bundled free in €29/year narrative.
2. **Absurdly low TCO** — unlimited users, firms, locations, invoices; no per-seat tax (directly attacks SmartBill/Saga pricing pain).
3. **Accounting export lane** — Saga, WinMentor, Ciel; „contabilul folosește ce vrea, tu Oblio” is a proven RO message.
4. **Stock + NIR for accountants** — plan de conturi alignment, FIFO, smart NIR, Oblio Wallet auto-import from supplier e-Factura (since 2017).
5. **Light production** — rețetar, bon producție, bon consum auto, transfer gestiuni — covers basic manufacturing/bakery without extra module fees.
6. **Casa de marcat bridge** — Datecs, Tremol, Daisy; numerar/card/tichete; barcode scanner; mobile printer — **fiscal bon**, not full hospitality POS.
7. **E-commerce integrations** — WooCommerce, PrestaShop, OpenCart, Shopify, eMAG (ro/bg/hu); free API.
8. **Social proof at scale** — 150k+ users, 97% „clienți fericiți”, testimonials from PFA to SRL.
9. **Onboarding speed** — 30s signup, 60s first invoice; import from SmartBill/FGO/EasyBill.
10. **Bizoo reviews** — cross-sell reputation layer after invoice/receipt.

### Where Oblio is thin (gaps vs franchisetech)

| Gap | Oblio | franchisetech advantage |
|-----|-------|-------------------------|
| **Hospitality POS** | Casa de marcat = fiscal receipt interface, not menu/till workflow | Full browser POS: categories, split pay, till session, drawer close |
| **Kitchen operations** | No KDS, no kitchen flow, no service-rush UX | Kitchen display, service-period ops |
| **Owner closing ritual** | Registru casă, jurnal bancă — accounting reports | Z-report, cash vs expected, owner summary „închide ziua cu adevărul” |
| **Recipe margin truth** | Production costing exists; no hospitality margin dashboard | Recipe costing tied to sales, margin reports for food SKUs |
| **Real-time service stock** | Stock reports; expiry „în curând” | Low-stock alerts during service, purchase→NIR flow for operators |
| **Staff / multi-role UX** | Unlimited users but no hospitality role model | Till permissions, guided mode, staff-focused UX |
| **FiscalNet / RO fiscal depth** | Hardware bridge to registered fiscal devices | FiscalNet local agent pattern for compliant RO tills |
| **Trial economics** | 1 year free → €29/year stickiness | 15-day trial, value proof in first sale + Z-report funnel |
| **Target ICP** | Any firm/PFA needing invoices | Food business owner: café, restaurant, takeaway, patiserie |

**Key insight:** Oblio wins when the buyer’s job is **„emit facturi și trimite în SPV”**. franchisetech wins when the job is **„știi ce s-a vândut, ce ai în stoc, și dacă casa se potrivește”**.

## Overlap & collision zones

| Area | Overlap level | Notes |
|------|---------------|-------|
| NIR / purchases | Medium | Both touch receptie marfa; Oblio is accountant-first, FT is operator-first |
| Stock / FIFO | Medium | Oblio for fiscal stock; FT for service-period depletion |
| Casa de marcat | High (surface) | Same hardware brands; different software depth |
| e-Factura | Low for FT | FT is not an e-Factura product; owners may use Oblio *alongside* |
| Registru de casă | Medium | Both offer cash register views; FT ties to POS session truth |
| Price sensitivity | High | Owner comparing €29/year vs €49/month will ask „de ce plătesc mai mult?” |

**Likely coexistence pattern:** Owner uses Oblio (or SmartBill) for B2B invoicing + SPV; franchisetech for daily till, kitchen, margins. Risk: owner assumes Oblio „face tot” because marketing bundles stock + casa de marcat free.

## Messaging angles for franchisetech

### When Oblio comes up

- **Don’t compete on invoice count or e-Factura.** Acknowledge Oblio is excellent and cheap for facturare.
- **Reframe:** „Oblio îți emite factura. franchisetech îți spune dacă ziua a fost profitabilă.”
- **Price justification:** €49/lună = less than one hour of owner confusion per month; vs €29/an for document emission only.
- **Hospitality proof:** till session → first sale → Z-report activation funnel; Oblio has no equivalent owner closing loop.
- **Anti-per-seat:** Both avoid per-user tax; parity on that objection.

### Objection handlers

| Owner says | Response direction |
|------------|------------------|
| „Oblio e €29 pe an” | „Perfect pentru facturi. Dar cine îți arată marja la cappuccino după serviciu?” |
| „Oblio are stoc și casă de marcat” | „Da — pentru bon fiscal și NIR contabil. Nu pentru fluxul de serviciu, bucătărie, închidere casă.” |
| „Contabilul folosește Saga” | „Exportăm rapoarte contabile; Oblio e pentru facturi, noi pentru operațiuni zilnice.” |
| „150.000 de utilizatori” | „Majoritatea PFA și facturare. Noi suntem construiți pentru cafenea/restaurant.” |

## Competitive threat level

| Dimension | Rating | Rationale |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| **Direct substitute** | Low | Different primary job-to-be-done |
| **Budget anchor** | **High** | €29/year sets „software should be almost free” expectation |
| **Feature confusion** | Medium | „Stocuri + casa de marcat gratuit” blurs POS boundary in owner mind |
| **Accounting channel** | Medium | Contabili may recommend Oblio; need partner narrative |
| **e-Factura mandate tailwind** | High (for Oblio) | Regulatory pull favors invoicing tools, not ops platforms |

**Overall:** Oblio is a **pricing and positioning reference**, not a head-to-head POS competitor. Treat as **adjacent stack** — partner or coexist, rarely replace.
