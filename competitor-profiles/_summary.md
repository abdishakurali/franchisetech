## Positioning matrix

## Client-pull differentiators (franchisetech)

1. **Total cost clarity** — Starter €49 includes sales, Z-style till close, and VAT reports. Ebriza Pro also lists €49 but Insights reporting is +€19/mo on public pricing. Oblio looks cheaper but is not a restaurant POS depth product.

2. **Operations in one browser** — POS, stock/NIR (Pro), recipe margins, kitchen display, till close without a marketplace of paid add-ons for basic reporting.

3. **Unlimited staff, per location** — Matches Ebriza/SmartBill multi-user; beats per-device POS licensing models.

4. **Honest coexistence with invoicing stacks** — Keep SmartBill/Oblio for e-Factura; use franchisetech for “did today’s service make money?”

5. **SMB activation vs enterprise sales** — Bit-Soft and Oracle paths need implementation; franchisetech targets 1–3 location cafés/restaurants with 15-day assisted trial.

## When NOT to push franchisetech

- **e-Factura-only** need at minimum cost → Oblio/SmartBill
- **Heavy delivery aggregator** volume with call-center ops → Ebriza Premium/Titanium or Bit-Soft Breeze
- **National franchise / hotel PMS** → Bit-Soft + Oracle ecosystem
- **Already deep in Ebriza** with paid integrations working → parallel trial, not rip-and-replace pitch
