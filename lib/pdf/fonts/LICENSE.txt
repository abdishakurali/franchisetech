Repackaged from @fontsource/roboto (latin + latin-ext subsets merged into a
single static TTF per weight) so Romanian diacritics render correctly in
@react-pdf/renderer, which cannot use the built-in Helvetica/Times/Courier
fonts for non-WinAnsi characters (ă â î ș ț).
