3. **Industries** — cafés, restaurants, takeaways (not enterprise chains — Bit-Soft owns that tier).
4. **Features grid on homepage** — six scannable tiles linking to `/features/*`.
5. **New compare pages** — `/compare/oblio`, `/compare/bit-soft`.
